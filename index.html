Trustless IoT Security

Use Case 1

Diagnostics and customer feedback automation

Client: A global blockchain company and smart appliance manufacturer

Customers of smart devices and appliances are incentivized to register their product through an app built on the IoTBlock Platform. Once registered, metadata from these devices will be logged on our blockchain over time and can be easily accessed by the manufacturer. When specific metadata is present, actions may be triggered such as running diagnostics software, generating diagnostic reports and sending them to a service provider, or launching a bounty to incentivize customer feedback (i.e. answering a survey or quiz, or submitting a photo of the damaged device).

Use Case 2

Car sharing network auditing automation

Client: A regional car sharing rental service

When a customer returns a car to a designated parking spot, the company knows the car is returned but does not know the condition of the car (i.e. whether it is scratched or damaged). Instead of hiring someone to audit the rental car, the company can launch a Public Bounty Request on an app built on the IoTBlock Platform to incentivize local app users to audit the car for the company (i.e. taking and submitting photos or videos of the car).

Use Case 3

Airline public health monitoring

Client: A global airline company

Airplanes are full of germs and disease vectors. To address this issue, an airliner wants to incentivize its passengers to help collect samples from the passenger area such as their seats and meal trays so that they can monitor the amount of germs present on the airplane over time. AI, Machine and Deep Learning then may be applied to better devise solutions to improve the public health and safety of airplanes. An app built on the IoTBlock Platform allows the airline to easily incentivize passengers to collect the samples, report health/sanitary issues or complaints, and ensure all the data is fingerprinted to the precise location within the airplane.

Use Case 4

Crypto mining management

Client: A dedicated crypto mining company in Singapore

There is currently a lack of secure, user friendly software to help crypto miners manage or monitor their operations. An app built on the IoTBlock Platform allows painless, remote crypto mining farm management powered by blockchain technology. Once miners register all their mining devices onto the app, the app may allow miners to receive live alerts when a specific miner has problems or if the wallet address has been changed by an unauthorized identity, switch the miner on/off, auto-restart the miner, log the history of a GPU's working life, and compile reports for a GPU card's history.
