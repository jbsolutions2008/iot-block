The Vision

Originating as an Internet of Things (IoT) electronics and hardware manufacturer since 2004, IoTBlock was founded in 2017 by a team of technologists and visionaries with a mission to promote the open, free, and secure exchange of IoT data on the decentralized web. Our goal is to develop the building blocks needed to accelerate the mass adoption of smart devices and assets similar to how web and mobile applications exploded at the early stages of the Internet.

We believe three catalysts are needed to achieve this goal:

Trustless Governance

There needs to be a trusted, open registry for IoT metadata governed by a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) that allows anyone to audit the health, safety, functional integrity, and security of all registered IoT assets.

Secure Data Interoperability

IoT data should be secure and useable across all architectures, blockchains, and smart contracts.
